SASLT Excellence Awardee 2023

Home Awards SASLT Excellence Awardee 2023

Prof. Waleed Alhamoudi

Professor of Medicine & Consultant

Gastroenterologist & Transplant Hepatologist

Prof. AlHamoudi is a well accomplished Professor of Medicine and Consultant Gastroenterologist & Hepatologist at King Saud University, Riyadh. He is also working as a Consultant Transplant Hepatologist, Liver and Small Bowel Health Center in King Faisal Specialist Hospital and Research Center, Riyadh. He is appointed as the Deputy Director and a Directive Board Member for the Liver Disease Research Center at King Saud University. He is an active member of Saudi Society for Organ Transplantation and a regular examiner for the Saudi Board of Internal Medicine. Prof. AlHamoud is a profound author and has been a member of several editorial boards such as Journal of Gastroenterology and Hepatobiliary Disorders, Clinical and Molecular Hepatology, SM Journal of Case reports, Austin Journal of Gastroenterology and World Journal of Hepatology. He published more than a hundreds of publications and abstracts. He participated in different committees and organization within and outside the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia.
