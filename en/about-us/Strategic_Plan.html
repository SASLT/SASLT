Strategic Plan

Home About Us Strategic Plan

Mission

Promote contribution to the advancement of science, spreading of knowledge and collaboration with health authorities in order to improve the standard of practice and enhance the efficiency regulations for the ultimate of providing best care to all liver patients within the Arab World.

Vision

Be the leading medical association in the Arab World for the advancement of science and enhancement of practice in the field of liver diseases.

Pillars

  • Excellence.
  • Collaboration.
  • Sustainability.
  • Innovation.

Objectives

To reach level A in SCFHs classification of medical societies. Develop/renew National Guidelines in the field of Hepatology. Expand social network footprint. Fund clinical research.
